  • History

    EXO debuted on April 8, 2012. They started with 12 members in the group and with 2 sub-group, EXO-K and EXO-M. Each sub-group have 6 members each. EXO-K promote the group in Korea while EXO-M promote the group in China. EXO-K consist of Suho, D.O., Chanyeol, Baekhyun, Kai and Sehun. EXO-M consist of Kris, Chen, Xiumin, Tao, Luhan and Lay. After Kris and Luhan left in 2014 and Tao left in 2015, the group left with 9 members.
